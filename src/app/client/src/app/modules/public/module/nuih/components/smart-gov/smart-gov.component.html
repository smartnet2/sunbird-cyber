Enabling integrated e-governance and digital delivery of civil services for ease of living

Mobile Enabled

Smart Governance will adopt a mobile first approach, with robust mobile applications enabling citizens to easily access government services through their phones. Government officials and field workers are also empowered with mobile applications that enable them to deliver 24Ã—7 governance with ease.




Real time Dashboards
to drive decisions

Real time dashboards provide governments with actionable insights on demand. Administrators and department heads are empowered with verified data that enables them to manage their work and budgets better. Field level employee reports enable effective performance management and ensures efficient usage of government resources.




Decision
Support Systems

Decision-Support Systems leverage the power of data and analytics to empower city leaders to solve fast-changing challenges, prioritize action and manage teams of execution. They can be used to improve mobility, disaster response, access to basic services, urban planning and other issues.




Ready to use

Smart governance platform will be configurable to local needs and can be quickly adopted across many cities at scale.






Open Standards and Open APIs

Smart Governance will be governed by the guiding principle of Open Standards and Open APIs which will enable inter-operability between multiple systems.
