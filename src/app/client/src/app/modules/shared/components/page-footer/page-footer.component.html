Ministry of Housing and Urban affairs launched SmartNet on 25th June 2016. SmartNet is an initiative of the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs to support the development of cities across India and to create a resource-rich ecosystem of learning, sharing and disseminating for city managers and primary stakeholders in the urban transformation of India. SmartNet encourages Government to Government (G-to-G) and Government to Business (G-to-B) and Business to Government (B-to-G) linkages for the urban sector in India enabling transparent and structured interactions between cities, businesses and institutions. SmartNet is currently managed by National Institute of Urban Affairs (NIUA).
